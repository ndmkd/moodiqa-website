Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Moodiqa does not collect any personal information from its users. The moodboards you create are processed entirely in your browser and are not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use your information for any purpose. Your moodboard creations remain private to your browser session.

Data Storage

All moodboard creation and editing happens locally in your web browser. We do not store any images or moodboards on our servers.

Cookies

Moodiqa does not use cookies or any form of tracking technology.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any information with third parties as we do not collect any user data.

Changes to This Policy
